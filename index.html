Global Duolingo English Test Acceptance Overview

The Duolingo English Test (DET) has revolutionized English proficiency testing for international students. With over 3,500 institutions worldwide now accepting DET scores, it has become a legitimate alternative to traditional tests like IELTS and TOEFL. For Indian students planning to study abroad in 2026, understanding which countries and universities accept DET is crucial for streamlining your application process.

Why DET is Gaining Global Popularity:
  • Convenience: Take the test from home in under 1 hour
  • Affordability: Only USD 65 (≈ ₹5,400) vs IELTS (₹16,500+)
  • Speed: Results in 48 hours vs weeks for traditional tests
  • Accessibility: Available 24/7 with no test center visits
  • Adaptive Format: Computer-adaptive testing for accurate assessment
  • Wide Recognition: Accepted by top universities globally

United States: Duolingo Accepted Universities

The United States has embraced the Duolingo English Test, with over 1,000 institutions now accepting DET scores. This includes Ivy League universities, state universities, and liberal arts colleges across all 50 states.

US Visa Note

US student visas (F-1) accept DET scores as proof of English proficiency if your university accepts it for admission. The I-20 form issued by your university is the key document.

United Kingdom: Duolingo Accepted Universities

The UK has a more selective approach to DET acceptance. While many universities accept DET for admission purposes, UK Visas and Immigration (UKVI) does not accept DET for visa applications from non-majority English speaking countries like India.

UK Universities Accepting DET for Admission

University DET Score Requirement Important Notes
Imperial College London 115-125 Selected postgraduate programs only; check department
London School of Economics (LSE) 120-130 Limited programs; mostly postgraduate
University of Bristol 110-120 Accepted for many undergraduate and postgraduate programs
University of Southampton 105-120 Widely accepted across faculties
Critical UK Visa Information

Indian students will still need an approved SELT (Secure English Language Test) like IELTS for UKVI or Pearson PTE Academic UKVI for the student visa application, even if your university accepts DET for admission.

Canada: Duolingo Accepted Universities

Canada is one of the most DET-friendly study destinations, with over 400 institutions accepting the test. Recent changes to Canadian visa policies have made DET even more attractive for Indian students.

Major Canadian Visa Update

The Canadian government has eliminated the Student Direct Stream (SDS) requirement that previously forced students to take IELTS. Now, if a Designated Learning Institution (DLI) accepts your Duolingo score and issues a Letter of Acceptance (LOA), this is generally sufficient proof of English proficiency for your study permit application.

Australia: Duolingo Accepted Universities

Australia's approach to DET is institution-specific rather than country-wide. While several universities accept DET for admission, the Department of Home Affairs does not accept DET for student visa applications.

Australian Universities Accepting DET

University DET Score Requirement Acceptance Scope
Australian National University (ANU) 115-125 Selected programs only; verify with department
Monash University 110-120 Limited acceptance; mostly postgraduate
University of Technology Sydney (UTS) 105-115 Several programs accept DET for admission
Macquarie University 100-110 Wider acceptance across faculties
Australian Visa Restriction

For Australian student visas (subclass 500), you must provide evidence of English proficiency from an approved test provider. DET is not on the approved list, so even if your university accepts DET for admission, you'll need to take an approved test (IELTS, TOEFL, PTE, or Cambridge) for your visa application.

Europe: Ireland, Germany & France DET Acceptance

European countries have varying approaches to DET acceptance, often depending on whether programs are taught in English and specific institutional policies.

Ireland

Good DET acceptance for English-taught programs at Irish universities.

  • University College Dublin: DET 110-120 for most programs
  • Trinity College Dublin: DET 115-125 (varies by program)
  • University of Galway: DET 105-115
Visa: Conditional

Irish visa authorities generally accept DET if the university does.

Germany

Limited DET acceptance mostly at private universities and specific programs.

  • Technical University of Munich: Selected international programs
  • Heidelberg University: Limited English-taught Master's
  • Private universities: Wider acceptance (check individually)
Visa: Check Required

German visa requirements vary; often need TestDaF or DSH.

France

Very limited DET acceptance primarily at business schools and international programs.

  • HEC Paris: MBA and select international programs
  • ESSEC Business School: English-taught programs
  • Sciences Po: Specific international relations programs
Visa: Not Accepted

French visas typically require TCF or DELF/DALF for French proficiency.

European Study Tip

For European countries, always verify directly with the university as DET acceptance can change frequently.

  • Public vs private institutions
  • English-taught vs local language programs
  • Undergraduate vs postgraduate levels

When in doubt, traditional tests (IELTS/TOEFL) are safer for European applications.

Visa Strategy for Indian Students

For countries that don't accept DET for visas (UK, Australia), you can still use DET for university admission if accepted, but will need to take an approved test for the visa. For Canada and US, DET can be used for both admission and visa if the university accepts it.

Pro Tips for Maximizing Your DET Success

Based on analysis of successful DET applicants across all countries, here are strategic tips for Indian students:

Country-Specific Strategy

For Canada & US: DET is optimal for both admission and visa. For UK & Australia: Use DET only if university accepts it, but plan for IELTS/other for visa.

Score Buffer Strategy

Aim for 10-15 points above the minimum requirement. Competitive programs often select students with higher scores even if they meet minimum.

Timing & Retakes

Take DET 3-4 months before application deadlines. You can retake it unlimited times (with 30-day wait after 3 attempts) to improve your score.

Verification Process

After getting your score, send it officially through Duolingo to each university. Don't just screenshot - use the official sending feature.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use the same DET score for both university admission and student visa?

It depends on the country. For the USA and Canada, yes - if your university accepts DET, it's generally sufficient for visa purposes too. For the UK and Australia, no - you'll need an approved test like IELTS for UKVI or an Australian-approved test for the visa, even if your university accepts DET for admission.

Which country has the widest DET acceptance for Indian students?

Canada and the United States have the widest DET acceptance. Canada has over 400 institutions accepting DET, and recent visa policy changes make it especially favorable. The US has over 1,000 institutions accepting DET, including top universities like Yale and Columbia. Both countries accept DET for student visa applications if the university accepts it for admission.

Is DET accepted for postgraduate (Master's/PhD) programs?

Yes, but check individual programs carefully. DET is widely accepted for postgraduate programs in the US and Canada. In the UK, acceptance is more limited to specific programs at universities like Imperial College London and LSE. Always verify with the specific department, as some competitive programs (especially PhDs) may have stricter requirements or prefer traditional tests.

How long is my DET score valid?

DET scores are valid for 2 years from the test date, similar to IELTS and TOEFL. This validity period is recognized by most universities worldwide. However, some institutions may have stricter policies, so always check the specific requirements of your target universities.
